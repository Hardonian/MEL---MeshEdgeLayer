# Why contribute to MEL

MEL is built for **operator truth** under degraded conditions: evidence-first semantics, explicit stale/degraded states, and separable trusted-control lifecycles. Contributing here means improving something field teams can **trust** — not painting over missing data.

## What you can ship that matters

- **Transport honesty** — clearer diagnostics, reconnection behavior, and labeling for supported paths only.
- **Incident and audit workflows** — surfaces that make persisted evidence and action attribution easier to use.
- **Docs and runbooks** — faster time-to-proof for the next operator (`docs/runbooks/`, `docs/getting-started/`).
- **Fixtures and scenarios** — reproducible regression and training data (`internal/demo/`, `make demo-verify`).

## Ground rules (short)

No fake transports, no collapsed “healthy” states, no canonical claims from assistive inference. Full contract: [AGENTS.md](../../AGENTS.md), [../product/HONESTY_AND_BOUNDARIES.md](../product/HONESTY_AND_BOUNDARIES.md).

# Common Misreads

## “Configured means live”
No. A transport can be configured but disconnected or stale.

## “Recommendation means MEL executed it”
No. Recommendations are advisory unless execution state proves dispatch/execution.

## “No incidents means healthy”
No. Empty queue can also mean missing/partial ingest.

## “Imported history proves current status”
No. Imported/offline data is context, not live evidence.

## “Guarded auto means full autonomy”
No. Guarded automation is bounded by policy, safety checks, and available actuators.

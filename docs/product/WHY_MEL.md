# Why MEL

MEL exists because operators need truthful systems during degraded operations.

## Problem

Generic dashboards flatten uncertainty and often hide degraded states.

## MEL answer

- Evidence-first runtime truth
- Explicit degraded/unknown semantics
- Trusted control with audited action lifecycle
- Proof-bearing incident workflows

## Buying rationale (honest)

Teams adopt MEL to reduce incident ambiguity, tighten operational trust, and preserve actionable memory over time.

# MEL GTM Strategy

**Date:** 2026-04-10  
**Status:** Active

---

## Target Market

### Primary: Field Operations Teams

**Persona:** Field ops managers, network engineers, industrial operators

**Pain Points:**
- Can't trust cloud-only dashboards under poor connectivity
- Need offline capability for remote sites
- Require audit trail for compliance
- Want evidence, not guesses

**Budget:** $2K-50K/year for tools

### Secondary: Government/Municipal

**Persona:** City ops, transportation, utilities

**Pain Points:**
- Regulatory compliance
- Local data hosting requirements
- Long procurement cycles

**Budget:** $50K-500K

## Messaging

### Core Positioning

> MEL is the **evidence-first operations OS** for teams that can't afford to trust cloud-only dashboards under field conditions.

### Key Messages

| Stakeholder | Message |
|-------------|---------|
| CTO | Evidence-first means audit-ready |
| Ops Manager | Works offline, shows truth |
| Field Operator | Reliable under pressure |
| Compliance | Complete audit trail |
| CFO | Predictable cost model |

### Not Messaging

- ❌ "Simple dashboard" (it's a professional tool)
- ❌ "AI-powered" (it's evidence-powered)
- ❌ "Cloud-native" (local-first)
- ❌ BLE/HTTP ingest (not supported)

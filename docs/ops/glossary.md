## Quick operator terms

- **Live**: recent ingest evidence is present.
- **Stale**: ingest evidence is older than freshness threshold.
- **Historical**: persisted past records, not current runtime proof.
- **Imported/Offline**: external evidence imported into MEL; not live proof by default.
- **Partial/Degraded**: known evidence or runtime gaps that must stay explicit.
- **Unsupported**: not implemented; do not assume partial behavior.

For preferred wording, discouraged legacy terms, support matrix language rules, and phrase examples, use the canonical guide.

## FAQ

### Q: What's counted as a "node"?

A: Any device running MEL that connects to the system. This includes:
- Physical edge devices
- Mobile devices
- Control plane servers

### Q: Can I run more than 5 nodes on Community?

A: Yes, but upgrade recommended for >5 nodes. Community is for evaluation.

### Q: Is there a per-user fee?

A: No. MEL pricing is per-node, not per-seat. This benefits large teams.

### Q: What's included in "local-first"?

A: MEL runs without cloud connectivity. Professional includes:
- Local database
- Local inference
- Offline operation
- Sync when online

### Q: How do upgrades work?

A: Purchase additional licenses or contact sales. Upgrades are:
- Prorated for new tiers
- Instant activation
- No migration required

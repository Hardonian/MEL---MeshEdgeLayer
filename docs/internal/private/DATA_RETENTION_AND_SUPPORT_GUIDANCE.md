# Data Retention and Support Guidance

## Retention posture

- Keep retention windows explicit per deployment policy.
- Preserve enough incident/action history to support RCA and recurring-failure analysis.
- Ensure export/delete procedures are documented and testable.

## Support posture

- Support requests should include bounded time windows and relevant IDs.
- Use redacted support bundle workflows by default.
- Treat imported/offline context as supplemental, not live proof.

## External review needed

- Legal review of retention timelines by jurisdiction.
- Privacy review for support bundle handling processes.

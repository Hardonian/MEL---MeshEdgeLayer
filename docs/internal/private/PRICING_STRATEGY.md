# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing thesis

MEL pricing should reflect **reduced incident ambiguity and faster trusted recovery**, not raw “dashboard seats.”

## Packaging anchors

- Community: OSS baseline and self-serve operation.
- Pro Operator: paid support + packaged operational leverage.
- Team/Incident Intelligence: deeper workflow governance and memory value.

## Anti-patterns (forbidden)

- Charging for basic truth semantics.
- Selling “AI autonomy” claims without actuator proof.
- Pricing detached from documented support boundaries.

# Design Partner Plan

## Objectives

- Validate first-hour to first-incident workflow clarity.
- Verify support/proofpack artifacts reduce escalation ambiguity.
- Collect outcome data for recommendation-memory roadmap.

## Candidate profile

- 1–3 operators per site.
- Recurring degraded connectivity conditions.
- Willingness to share anonymized incident outcomes.

## Exit criteria

- At least 3 incident cycles completed with artifact exports.
- Operator trust survey indicates improved confidence in what is known vs unknown.
- Identified roadmap items mapped to maintenance/leverage/moat classes.

# Buyer Objections and Answers

## “Is this just another dashboard?”
No. MEL differentiates on evidence semantics, trusted control lifecycle, and proofpack/support workflows.

## “Do you do mesh routing?”
No. MEL does not claim RF routing execution; it is an incident-intelligence and trusted-control layer.

## “Can this run without cloud?”
Yes for core operation. Optional integrations are additive, not mandatory.

## “Is recommendation output autonomous?”
No. Recommendation surfaces are advisory and bounded by uncertainty.

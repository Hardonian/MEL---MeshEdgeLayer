# Commercial Terms Notes

Operational notes only; requires legal review before use in contracts.

## Suggested defaults (non-binding)

- Subscription period: annual preferred, monthly allowed for pilots.
- Support tiers tied to response windows and support artifact quality.
- Explicit exclusions for unsupported transports/features.

## Required legal review topics

- liability caps
- data processing addendum requirements
- export controls (if applicable)
- governing law/jurisdiction

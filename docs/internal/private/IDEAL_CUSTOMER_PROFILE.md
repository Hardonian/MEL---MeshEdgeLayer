# Ideal Customer Profile

## ICP v1

Organizations that:

- operate local/mixed-channel deployments under degraded conditions,
- require operator-trustworthy evidence and incident history,
- cannot accept mandatory-cloud or privacy-weak defaults.

## Disqualifiers

- buyers seeking turnkey RF routing control not in MEL scope,
- buyers needing immediate formal compliance attestation,
- teams unwilling to run local operational discipline.

# Incident Proofpack and Exports

## Why this exists

Proofpacks are MEL’s primary mechanism for proving what happened without overclaiming certainty.

## Minimum proofpack contents

- Incident identity and time window.
- Supporting transport/status evidence.
- Action lifecycle records (if any).
- Operator notes and caveats.
- Explicit unknown/degraded markers.

## Export workflow

1. Identify incident scope.
2. Export proofpack/support bundle.
3. Validate redaction/privacy settings.
4. Attach artifacts to ticket or handoff record.

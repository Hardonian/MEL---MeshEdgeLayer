# Privacy and Data Posture

## Baseline posture

- Local-first operation with no mandatory cloud dependency.
- Export redaction and privacy audit surfaces are built into operations.
- Assistive inference is optional and non-canonical.

## Data posture rules

- Distinguish live local evidence from imported/offline context.
- Preserve retention/delete/export semantics explicitly.
- Do not market privacy guarantees beyond implemented controls.

## Required external review

Before commercial scale claims:

- legal review of privacy notices/terms
- jurisdiction-specific retention review
- security review for deployment-specific secret management
